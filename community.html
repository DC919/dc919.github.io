DCGs are not intended to compete with any other computer groups, rather to provide another gathering place for the discussion of technology and security topics.
We also seek to provide some cohesion to the local hacker community by providing awareness of other groups.
We contribute to conferences and hold other special events.

The Electronic Frontier Foundation is the leading nonprofit organization defending civil liberties in the digital world. Founded in 1990, EFF champions user privacy, free expression, and innovation through impact litigation, policy analysis, grassroots activism, and technology development.
