Def Con Group 919

We are the DEF CON Group (DCG) for the RTP/RDU/Raleigh/Durham/Chapel Hill area of North Carolina.
If you are not in this area, please find a DCG (or start one!) closer to you by visting: defcongroups.org/dcpages.html


Current schedule for meetings can be found on Meetup.com DEF CON 919
The group is private and there are questions to join the group only to screen out people that think they're signing up for the Las Vegas conference. Which we are not.

Community

DCGs are not intended to compete with any other computer groups, rather to provide another gathering place for the discussion of technology and security topics.
We also seek to provide some cohesion to the local hacker community by providing awareness of other groups.
